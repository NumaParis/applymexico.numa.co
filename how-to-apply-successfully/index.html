How to Apply

Apply now on Fundacity

This questionnaire is not to determine if your project is suitable or not. It is simply to help us adjust the type of
support and allocate the best resources for you based on your specific needs.

Don't be afraid if you encounter a question that you are not sure how to answer, just be honest and simply say
that you do not yet have the answer at this stage, it will not impact your chances of getting in, on the contrary it will help us give you more support in that specific area.

It might seem like a lot of work, but these questions will actually help you define important aspects of your project
that you will surely encounter along the way sooner or later. So, why not start today?

This application will be reviewed by at least 3 experts from different fields (business, design and tech). These are
experienced mentors, investors, tech experts, corporate directors and the team at NUMA.

Please answer the questions in english and be complete and concise in your answers. There is no need to complete
the form in one go, it is automatically saved and you can always come back and complete it later. If you have any
questions or trouble completing the application, please submit your question on the "Discuss" tab at the top of the application so that everyone can benefit from the answer.
